History of RockUs

This summer, while relaxing on the beach, I looked at the sand on my crocs and noticed an interesting grain of sand, it was very similar to amogus. I remembered that the amogus nugget were recently sold, so I decided to put my own on the Ebay. The auction was successful, with an initial bid of $ 100, the price rose to $ 11,200, but due to problems with PayPal, the transaction did not take place. And so I decided to sell digital versions of my amogus made of stone or shell (hence the name RockUs). In addition, I will present a pleasant surprise to the first owner of the most expensive NFT from the collection.

RockUs Collections

All RockUs NFT are divided into several collections. They differ in rarity and number of copies. There are three levels of rarity in total: common, rare and legendary.

Bored RockUs

Common NFT - nothing special, but they look cool and cost a little. The best option for fans of cool NFT's.

Rainbow RockUs

Rare NFT - it won't leave anyone indifferent. Created based on Andy Warhol's paintings. A total of 70 copies were produced, 10 for each color of the rainbow.

God of RockUs

Legendary NFT.
God - and that says it all. This NFT is released in a single copy, it is intended only for the true connoisseur. Only one copy was released. The first owner of this NFT will receive an amazing gift)))
